Baking Showcase!

Over the course of quarantine and my Zoom semesters at home, I picked up baking as a newfound passion. I believe it highlights many of my strengths: determination, punctuality, and creativity.

From recreating a recipe til I get it right, to developing my own recipes and tweaks, baking has been a hand-on passion of mine that helps me explore my creative side.

Food is something most of us can bond over. Below you'll fine mooncakes, baked completely from scratch, shared over the Mid-Autumn Festival with my family, and baked cookies sent to friends I had not seen for month. I am grateful for this passion of mine and how it has helped me express my creativity, all While helping me continue to connect and build relationships in my life.
